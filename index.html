Clarity Window Cleaners

Window cleaning with purified water


The windows have to be thoroughly cleaned. To do this, water fed extendable pole uses telescopic poles complete with cleaning brushes that can easily access heights of up to 70-feet. The pure water is then pumpes up these poles, which allows the window cleaner to scrub and rinse away any dirt. Because the cleaning process has used pure water, the windows can then be left to dry naturally, with a perfect, crystal clear finish.
